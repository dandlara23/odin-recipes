Carbonara Pasta

What is Carbonara Pasta

Carbonara pasta is a pasta that typically features a creamy suace that is made with pancetta, garlic, pasta water, cheese and eggs.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. kosher salt
  • 4 oz. guanciale, pancetta, or bacon
  • 2 oz. Parmesan or Pecorino Romano
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp. ectra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. spaghetti. bucatini, or rigatoni

Steps

  1. Heat 6 qt. water in a large pot over high heaty. When water starts to steam, add 3 tbsp. salt and cover pot with a lid.

  2. While yopu are waiting on the water, do a little prep. Cut 4 oz. of yopur desired meat and cut into 1" strips.

  3. Finely grate 2 oz. cheese and set aside one quarter of cheese for later

  4. Whisk 4 egg yolks and 2 whole eggs in a medium bowl until no streaks remain, then stir in remaining ghrated cheese.

  5. Working next to pot, heat 2 tbsp. oil in a large pot over medium. Add sliced meat and cook for 7-10 minutes.

  6. Remove pot from heat. Using a wooden spoon, fish out meat and transfer to small bowl. Pour fat into heatproof measuring cup, then add back about 3 tbsp. to pot. Discard remaining fat

  7. Cook 1 lb. of pasta in boiling water, stiriing occasionally, 2 minutes shy of package instructions. Just before pasta is finished, scoop out 1 3/4th cups of pasta cooking liquid with heatproof measuring cup.

  8. Add 1 cup reserved pasta cooking liquid to pot and bring to boil over medium-high heat. Drain pasta in a colander, then transfer to pot.

  9. Cook pasta, stiriing constantly unitl water is reduced by half. Remove from heat.

  10. Whisk 1/4th cup reserved pasta water into egg mixture, then very slowly stream into Dutch oven, stirring constantly, until cheese is melted and egg is thickened to form a glossy sauce.
